What are this?

Hey there, I'm Ploe. This chunk of data and logic is my website. It's an online lit-zine. A creative writing site with a twist. You submit your content. I decide whether it's good enough to feature on the front page.

That's not an overly interesting twist...

Yup, yup - but here is where it gets pretentious. Each month I will start with an editorial. A piece of writing with a certain set of vibes. It's then your turn to churn out some prose, verse, article or otherwise that compliments these vibes. Think of it as a story tag where the delivery isn't bound to a single form. We unpack our ideas across this digital zine and see what we end up with.

Unless your content gets featured it'll be purged from the site. I want your pieces of writing to be thematically related, each individual piece feeding from the previous in to the next. Notions filtering through. Some class of abstract lore should form with each iteration.

What's in it for me?

Presently? Fuck all... But it'll be a lark. Won't it?


Rules

These must be followed otherwise we'll suspend your account.

  • Do not blatantly advertise.
  • Do not include illegal content in your work.

Suspension is at Ploe's discretion, so be wary.

Guidelines
  • Pretension eases the tension.
  • If your critique of another's work is 'tl;dr' you're doing it wrong - read it. It's what we're here for.
  • When in doubt: look it up!
  • If you feature visual artwork ensure that it's your own, otherwise we won't feature it.

Mechanics

Here's how you play...

  • Log in by registering your email address
  • Read the editorial and featured articles
  • Read and critique the other users' submissions
  • Wait for it...
  • BAM! Write something
